Major Mahmud is not just a consultant or advisor - he‚Äôs the person people call when things feel impossible. A seasoned Army Officer, he served in the Bangladesh Army, including as Commanding Officer of Border Guard Bangladesh (BGB), where he led over 900 troops through high-pressure operations across the country.
